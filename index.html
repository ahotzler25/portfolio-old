About Me

I'm Andy Hotzler, a full-stack software engineer located in northern Minnesota with my domestic shorthair, Dahlia. I specialize in React and enjoy studying UX/UI design. Two heads are always better than one, and I enjoy reviewing code with colleagues to not only see how it can be improved, but also how different approaches can reach a solution. Working alone has advantages, but collaborating with a dedicated team cannot be matched.


Clients understand that a poorly designed, functional app--even if it's game-changing--will only be half as successful as it could be if it were well-designed. As a designer and a developer, my job is to 1) help clients attain their wants/needs, and 2) make their project look good.


After graduating from Bemidji State University with three Bachelors and a minor, I began relearning web development and followed my interests into game and software development. After almost two years of Udemy courses and tutorial hell on YouTube and freecodecamp.org, I felt like I was only placing pieces of a much larger puzzle together while being unable to see the larger picture. After researching bootcamps, curriculums, and job markets in Minneapolis and Winnipeg, I made the commitment enrolled in Thinkful's engineering immersion program.


When I'm not coding or studying new languages/frameworks, I can be found butchering the German language, running it down Summoner's Rift in League of Legends, saving Tamriel in Elder Scrolls Online, or incorrectly sequencing my plays in Magic: The Gathering.
