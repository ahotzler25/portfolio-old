About Me

I'm Andy Hotzler, a full-stack software engineer located in northern Minnesota with my domestic shorthair, Dahlia.


After graduating from Bemidji State University with three Bachelors and a minor, I began relearning web development and followed my interests into game and software development. After almost two years of Udemy courses and tutorial hell on YouTube and freecodecamp.org, I felt like I was only placing pieces of a much larger puzzle together while being unable to see the larger picture. After researching bootcamps, curriculums, and job markets in Minneapolis and Winnipeg, I made the commitment to programming and enrolled in Thinkful's engineering immersion program.


When I'm not coding or studying new languages/frameworks, I can be found butchering the German language, running it down Summoner's Rift in League of Legends, saving Tamriel in Elder Scrolls Online, or incorrectly sequencing my plays in Magic: the Gathering.
